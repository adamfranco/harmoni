Workflow Open Service Interface Definition.

The Workflow Open Service Interface Definition (OSID) shares some concepts with Authorization. It uses Roles and Qualifiers , referencing them by Id, but not providing a persistent store interface for these objects. If this is needed the Authorization OSID can provide this. In other words you would establish and maintain who had what role through Authorization, In this way the authorization and the workflow definitions can be the same. This is often the case; a person is assigned to do a certain step and also must have the authorization to do it. Another way to think about qualifierId is that it is an external reference to something which might have a bunch of properties. (Examples; CourseSectionId, ...)
