Grading Open Service Interface Definition.

The Grading Open Service Interface Definition (OSID) supports storing and retrieving GradeRecords and GradeObjects. A GradeRecord is specified by:

  • AgentId
  • GradableObjectId
  • GradeRecordType
  • GradeType
  • GradeValue
A GradableObject is specified by:
  • Id
  • CourseSectionId
  • ExternalReferenceId
  • GradeScale
  • GradeWeight
  • ScoringDefinition
This approach provides a general and flexible way to characterize a Grade.

A note about the relationship between the Grading OSID and the Assessment OSID: The Assessment OSID concerns the definition, management, and evaluation of Assessments. The evaluation component of the Assessment OSID is distinct and most likely more complex than a GradeRecord, however it may be the case that an Assessment element is the GradableObject referred to in GradeGrade.
